MY INTERESTS

Travel and Plane Spotting

I love travelling and plane spotting. It is really sad that I haven't travelled in a while due to the Covid situation in mainland China, but I guess things will be getting better soon.


Gaming

I love playing simulation games such as Microsoft Flight Simulator, Prepar3D and Cities: Skylines. One thing I like about flight simulators is that I get to fly different aircrafts, learning about the various design and concepts behind those aircrafts...My favorite plane flown so far is the Airbus A300-600/A310 series, and its cockpit is a perfect combination of the classic analog instruments with the modern glass cockpit!


Computer Systems

I am broadly interested in computer systems, including databases, operating systems and so on. I think it is pretty natural for me to be interested in this field, since I like studying different components of a system, how they interact with each other, just like when I study the cockpit of a particular aircraft, how different components of the system work together to make the airplane fly.
